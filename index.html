About us

Located 30 seconds from the main beach, La Comuna offers everything you need to enjoy your holidays. You can purchase clothes and swimwear for the whole family, pool floats, essential sanitary products, and a variety of beach snacks and drinks.
And when it is time for you to leave us, we have a variety of souvenirs available so you can take back home a piece of Mallorca with you.
